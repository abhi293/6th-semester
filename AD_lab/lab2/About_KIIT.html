About KIIT University

KIIT (Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology) is a multi-disciplinary university located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, medicine, law, and more.

The university is recognized for its modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and vibrant campus life. It is ranked among the top institutions in India and has a strong global presence.
