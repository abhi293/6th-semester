Inception

About the Movie

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, featuring Leonardo DiCaprio as a thief with the ability to enter people's dreams and steal secrets from their subconscious.

Cast

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
