Leonardo DiCaprio

Biography

Leonardo DiCaprio is an Academy Award-winning actor known for his performances in films like *Titanic*, *Inception*, *The Revenant*, and *The Wolf of Wall Street*. He is also an environmental activist, working on projects to combat climate change.

Filmography

  • Inception
  • Titanic
  • The Revenant
  • The Wolf of Wall Street

Awards and Achievements

  • Academy Award for Best Actor (2016) - *The Revenant*
  • Golden Globe Awards for Best Actor - *The Wolf of Wall Street* (2014) and *The Revenant* (2016)
